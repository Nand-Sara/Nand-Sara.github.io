Famous cuisines of India

Jan 10, 2023

Food is the way to someone's heart. Good food has the power to make anyone happy and healthy. India is known for having a diverse cuisine with dishes that have origins from various cultures and ethnicities. The food that is cooked with select spices, has an aroma that wafts in the air, making our mouths water. The careful combination of spices, pulses, grains and vegetables make the dishes tasty and nutritious.
Here are some dishes that are widely popular throughout India.

Food may be essential as fuel for the body, but good food is fuel for the the soul.

- Malcom Forbes

Breakfast Idli

Idli (can be spelled as 'idly'), is a South Indian dish that is commonly eaten as breakfast. Idli, otherwise known as steamed rice cakes, is prepared by grinding soaked Urad Dal and Idli Rice into a batter. The batter is allowed to ferment and the fermented batter is steamed into soft, bouncy idlis and is accompanied with Sambhar or Chutney.

Biryani

Biryani is one of the most popular dishes in India. The rice chosen and the preparation method varies with each locale, but the outcome is always delectable - an aromatic plate of steaming biryani. Cooking is an art and the key to a successful biryani is the paste or powder used to bring the flavour, texture and aroma to the dish. A range of dried spices namely, cloves, cardamon, star anise, cinnamon, black peppercorn and bay leaves are ground into a powder or a fine paste and mixed with the boiled rice. The cooked rice is then topped with vegetables, seafood or meat as per preference and is served hot.

Chicken Tikka

Another famous dish for the meat lovers, the Chicken Tikka. Pieces of marinated, boneless chicken are roasted in a tandoor, where the meat is placed in the oven on long metal skewers. The result is a smoky, reddish, juicy dish that is served with Pudina Chutney.
