Spring Fashion Trends 2023

Feb 16, 2023

Be fashion ready this Spring with the latest trends to celebrate this season. Sport the colors of spring and choose breathable fabrics that can keep you comfortable while still being a fashion icon. From a flowy midi dress, a floral top, to baggy pants, a knitted poncho and a trendy rain coat to beat the spring showers.


Spring adds new life and new beauty to all that is.

- Jessica Harrelson

Flowy Midi Dress

You can never go wrong with a free-flowing dress. Whether it has stripes, polka dots, plain pastels or bright colors, a midi dress paired with a hat is the best outfit combo for casual spring wear.

Knitted Poncho

A poncho can be combined with any top and a pair of jeans. Handmade or store bought, a poncho is a fluid piece of clothing that can be worn by anyone to give a complete boho look.
